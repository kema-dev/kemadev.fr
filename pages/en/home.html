What is important to me : Making wise choices

World is evolving incredibly fast, offering more and more powerful tools everyday, but those tools could become insidious weapons. We all need to be aware of this threat and act towards a safer deployment of technologies.

"AI is a tool. The choice about how it gets deployed is ours" - Oren Etzioni

What is important to me : Protecting privacy

Previously mentioned technologies are exploited to gently lurk into our lives, extracting as much data as possible to profile people and lead them into directions that suits exploiters' interests. It is now vital to spread good privacy practices to ensure that people are not mislead and therefore establish a stable democracy.

"Arguing that you don't care about the right to privacy because you have nothing to hide is no different than saying you don't care about free speech because you have nothing to say" - Edward Snowden

What is important to me : Sharing knowledge

Making tools accessible to anyone by promoting free and open-source is a great way to spread knowledge to anyone interested.
